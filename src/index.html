WHAT IS
HIDDEN SURPLUS?

Hidden Surplus is a way to rethink how to put your money to a greater cause by recognizing that small sacrifices can bring big change, and it is a way to track these changes.

Imagine if everyone decided to give up one item or service that they regularly purchase but do not actually need (what we refer to as our hidden surplus) and donated that money instead. We do not want you to give these things up indefinitely, just here and there, whether it's once a month or once a year.

Hidden Surplus is a way to give donations without having to pull out extra funds from your finances, making donating very doable. Hidden Surplus challenges everyone to give up a luxury, put that money towards a good cause, and fill out the "Do Good" form, so everyone can see the amazing change being made!
